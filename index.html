Mini SpaceX Info Page

SpaceX is a private aerospace company. The company was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk. The main purpose for Elon Musk to create SpaceX was to colonize Mars. The company had to go through some failures at the beginning to become an innovating company.

At the beginning SpaceX had three failed launches. The failed launches put SpaceX's future in danger due to the fact that funding would be retracked, but the company's fourth was a success. This success allowed the company to secure funding to further their future flights. After successfully launching a number of rockets the company, started to work on landing their rockets after being launched in order to use them to lower the cost of space travel. There were a number of failed landings of the rockets, but in 2016 they successfully landed one of their rockets.
